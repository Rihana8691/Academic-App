# Academic App 🎓

A comprehensive, all-in-one personal academic coach and lifestyle tracker built with React Native and Expo. Designed specifically for students to manage their educational goals, daily spiritual habits, and personal well-being.

## ✨ Features

### 📚 Academic Management
- **Smart Dashboard**: At-a-glance view of your current GPA, earned credits, and pending tasks.
- **Academic Year Grouping**: Organize your semesters by year (Year 1 to Year 6) with expandable sections to keep your history clean.
- **Goal Coach**: Set target grades for each course. The app calculates your "Mistake Points" and tells you exactly how much you can afford to lose while still meeting your goal.
- **Hybrid Course Support**: Track separate schedules and grades for **Theory** and **Lab** sections within the same course.
- **Timeline Schedule**: Visual daily timeline that automatically removes past classes to keep you focused on what's next.

### 📝 Study & Task Tracking
- **Syllabus Mastery**: Break down your courses into specific topics and track your "Exam Readiness" percentage.
- **Task Manager**: Checklist for assignments and deadlines with real-time countdowns.
- **Exam Countdown**: Smart sequential tracking that focuses on your Mid Exam first, then automatically switches to your Final Exam.

### 🌙 Lifestyle & "My Day"
- **Prayer Tracker**: Daily checklist for the 5 prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha).
- **Habit Tracker**: Daily check-ins for "No Sugar," "Reading," and "Studying."
- **Personal Journal**: A private space to record daily thoughts and reflections.
- **Historical View**: Go back in time using the calendar to review or update past habits and prayers.

### 🎨 Design & Experience
- **Premium Dark Mode**: OLED-optimized dark theme with high-contrast teal accents for reduced eye strain during late-night study sessions.
- **Modern UI**: Clean, card-based interface with intuitive tabbed navigation for complex course details.
- **Data Safety**: Professional database migration logic ensures you never lose your records during app updates.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Framework**: React Native (Expo)
- **Database**: SQLite (Local Storage)
- **Navigation**: React Navigation
- **Icons**: Expo Vector Icons (Ionicons, MaterialCommunityIcons)

## 📱 Installation (Personal APK Build)
1. Install EAS CLI: `npm install -g eas-cli`
2. Login: `eas login`
3. Build APK: `eas build -p android --profile preview`
